# Access Control Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** June 2026  
**Owner:** Information Security Team  
**Review Date:** June 2027  
**Classification:** Internal  

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines the requirements for controlling access to systems, applications, and data within the organisation. It ensures that access is granted based on business need, reviewed regularly, and removed promptly when no longer required.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, and third parties who access organisation systems, applications, or data.

---

## 3. Principles

**3.1 Least Privilege**
Users are granted the minimum level of access required to perform their job function. Access is never granted by default.

**3.2 Need to Know**
Access to sensitive data is restricted to individuals who require it to perform their role.

**3.3 Separation of Duties**
Critical functions are divided between multiple individuals to reduce the risk of fraud or error.

**3.4 Default Deny**
Access is denied by default. Access must be explicitly requested, approved, and granted.

---

## 4. Access Request Process

1. User or manager submits an access request
2. Request is approved by the user's line manager
3. IT or security team provisions the access
4. Access is documented in the access register
5. User is notified of access granted

---

## 5. User Account Management

**5.1 Account Creation**
- Accounts are created only upon receipt of an approved access request
- Each user must have a unique account
- Shared accounts are not permitted except where technically unavoidable

**5.2 Account Modification**
- Changes to access levels require a new approved access request
- Role changes trigger an access review within 5 business days

**5.3 Account Suspension**
- Accounts are suspended immediately upon employee termination or extended leave
- Suspended accounts are reviewed after 30 days and deleted if no longer required

**5.4 Account Deletion**
- Accounts are deleted within 24 hours of confirmed termination
- Data associated with deleted accounts is retained per the data retention policy

---

## 6. Privileged Access

- Privileged accounts are issued only to authorised IT and security staff
- Privileged access is used only for tasks requiring elevated permissions
- Privileged account activity is logged and reviewed monthly
- Privileged accounts are reviewed quarterly

---

## 7. Third Party Access

- Third party access is granted only for the duration of the engagement
- Third party accounts are reviewed monthly
- Third parties must sign a data processing agreement before access is granted
- Third party access is removed immediately upon contract end

---

## 8. Access Reviews

- All user access is reviewed quarterly
- Privileged access is reviewed monthly
- Access reviews are documented and signed off by the relevant manager
- Findings from access reviews are actioned within 10 business days

---

## 9. Remote Access

- Remote access to organisation systems requires VPN
- MFA is mandatory for all remote access
- Remote access sessions are logged and monitored

---

## 10. Policy Violations

Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment or contract.
